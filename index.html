About the Conference


National Conference on Science, Engineering & Management is a prominent academic and professional gathering that brings together researchers, scholars, and industry experts to explore recent advancements and innovative practices in the fields of system analysis, computer science, and their real-world applications. The conference serves as a platform for presenting cutting-edge research, exchanging ideas, and discussing future trends in areas such as software engineering, data analysis, artificial intelligence, and computational modelling. Through keynote sessions, technical presentations, and interactive discussions, the event fosters collaboration and knowledge sharing among participants from diverse backgrounds.

Conference Topics

  • System Modelling and Simulation, Software Engineering and System Design
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Applications, Data Analytics and Decision Support Systems
  • Cyber security and Information Assurance, Cloud Computing and Distributed Systems
  • Human-Computer Interaction and Usability Engineering, Internet of Things (IoT) and Embedded Systems
  • Big Data Management and Processing, Computer Vision and Image Processing
  • Applied Sciences, Business Management
  • Communication Networks, Circuits and Systems
  • Drug Discovery and Health Informatics, Data Science and Advanced Computing
  • Industrial Automation, Information Technology
  • Nano Science, Multimedia
  • Robotics & Automation, Sensor Technology
  • Structural Health Monitoring and GIS, VLSI
